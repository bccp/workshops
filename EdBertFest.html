EdBertFest at Vila Vipolže

As a special event of this year's workshop we are going to celebrate Ed Bertschinger's 60th birthday together with his (former) students, post docs and collaborators.

The festivities will span two days, with related scientific talks on Thursday and Friday and the main event on Friday afternoon at Vila Vipolže, a beautiful Renaissance Villa surrounded by vineyards.

The celebration on Friday afternoon is part of the workshop's program. If you would like to join us for the event at Vila Vipolže only and are not interested in the remainder of the workshop, you can indicate so in the registration form.

Note that while we organize accommodation for participants of the workshop, we can unfortunately not provide additional accommodation for those that only attend the event on Friday afternoon.
